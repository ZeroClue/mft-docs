## Why Transparent Pricing?

Because "contact sales" is a barrier, not a feature.

DevOps teams should be able to evaluate tools without:
- Scheduling a demo call
- Waiting for a sales representative
- Negotiating opaque contracts

Our pricing is published. Our features are documented. Our CLI is free to try.

## FAQ

**Why publish pricing?**
We believe in transparent, developer-friendly purchasing. No gatekeepers, no hidden fees.

**What counts as a transfer?**
One transfer = one file movement from source to destination, regardless of file size.

**Can I upgrade/downgrade?**
Yes. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

**What if I exceed my transfer limit?**
We'll notify you at 80% and 100%. You can upgrade or purchase additional transfers.

**Do you offer free trials?**
Design partners get free access. Post-launch, we offer a 14-day trial with 100 transfers.

**What payment methods?**
Credit card (Starter, Pro) and invoice (Enterprise).
