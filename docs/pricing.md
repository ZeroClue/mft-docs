## FAQ

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes. Community tier is free forever. Starter and Pro include a 14-day trial with full feature access.

**What counts as a transfer?**
One transfer = one file movement from source to destination, regardless of file size.

**Can I upgrade/downgrade?**
Yes. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

**What if I exceed my transfer limit?**
We'll notify you at 80% and 100%. You can upgrade or purchase additional transfers.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Credit card (Starter, Pro) and invoice (Enterprise). Community is free.

**Do you offer discounts for non-profits or open source?**
Yes. Contact us for special pricing.

**What's included in priority support?**
Pro tier gets response within 4 business hours. Enterprise gets dedicated support with SLA guarantees.

**Can I self-host?**
Yes. Enterprise tier includes on-premise deployment option with full support.
